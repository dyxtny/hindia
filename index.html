About Hindia

So much can be said about Hindia, some of which is summed up by Doves, ’25 on Canvas, the latest release from the Indonesian game changer. In this surprise mixtape, he expands his sonic canvas with the help of new collaborators including electro pop duo White Chorus and his live backing band, Blue Valley Radio. Meanwhile, his lyrics remain as deeply personal as ever, acting as a journal that navigates the loss of his beloved cats on “kids” and “betty”, broken relationships on “aku berharap ini tak terjadi padamu”, and the broken state of the nation on “hated in the nation” and “letdown”.
